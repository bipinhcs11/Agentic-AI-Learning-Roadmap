# Acme 401(k) Reference Summary — 2026

Acme uses this public-source summary for educational 401(k) questions. Figures are
summarized from IRS and Fidelity references and are not individualized advice.

## Employee contribution limits

For 2026, the employee salary-deferral limit is **$24,500**. This is the limit for
the employee's own traditional and Roth 401(k) deferrals across plans.

## Combined employee + employer limit

The separate overall annual additions cap is **$72,000** for 2026 before age-based
catch-up amounts. This includes employee deferrals, employer matching dollars, and
other plan contributions.

## Acme match context

The mock Acme plan matches 100% of the first 3% of pay and 50% of the next 3% of
pay. A 6% employee contribution reaches the full mock employer match.
