# Acme HSA Reference Summary — 2026

Acme uses this public-source summary for educational HSA questions. The figures are
summarized from IRS Revenue Procedure 2025-19 and Fidelity references.

## 2026 contribution limits

For 2026, the HSA contribution limit is **$4,400** for self-only coverage and
**$8,750** for family coverage. Eligible account holders age 55 or older can add a
**$1,000** catch-up contribution.

## HDHP eligibility

For 2026, an HSA-qualified HDHP must meet minimum deductible and maximum
out-of-pocket rules. Medicare enrollment generally stops new HSA contributions.

## Triple tax advantage

HSA contributions can be pre-tax or deductible, growth can be tax-free, and
qualified medical withdrawals can be tax-free. This is educational only and not tax
or investment advice.
